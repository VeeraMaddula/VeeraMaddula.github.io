// About Me

Building Reliable Systems at Scale

I'm a DevOps Engineer with 3+ years of hands-on experience building, automating, and managing CI/CD pipelines, cloud infrastructure, and containerized application deployments for large-scale, globally distributed, multi-tenant SaaS production systems on AWS.

I've worked with Disney Interactive supporting e-commerce platforms serving millions of daily transactions across four global regions. Over my career, I've supported 25+ digital products and multiple e-commerce websites — each built as independent microservices written in diverse programming languages including Java, Python, Go, and Node.js, requiring deep cross-stack operational expertise.

I've resolved 300+ high-priority production tickets and executed numerous change tasks across these polyglot microservice environments, ensuring minimal downtime and rapid incident recovery. My passion lies in automation-first culture, infrastructure as code, and empowering development teams to ship faster and more reliably.

Currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Computing at South East Technological University in Waterford, Ireland, while seeking new opportunities to contribute to cutting-edge cloud-native teams.
